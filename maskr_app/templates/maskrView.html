About Our Application

Image Masking with AI

Our application uses artificial intelligence to generate masks for user images based on the objects and landscapes detected in the image. With just a few clicks, you can easily select the mask that you want to save.

Easy to Use

Our application is designed to be user-friendly and easy to use, even if you have no experience with image processing or AI. Simply upload your images and let our system do the rest!
